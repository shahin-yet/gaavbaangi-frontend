About Gaav Baangi Conservation

The Gaav Baangi Conservation Project is dedicated to protecting and preserving the natural habitats and migration patterns of the Gaav Baangi species. Our interactive mapping system helps conservationists, researchers, and local communities track and manage conservation efforts effectively.

This platform provides tools for:

  • Mapping protected areas and migration routes
  • Monitoring population trends
  • Coordinating conservation activities
  • Sharing data with stakeholders
